01 / about

Who I am

I'm a web developer based in Portugal, currently between jobs and using the time well — reading low-level programming books, sharpening my C, and slowly befriending Rust. I believe the best way to understand high-level abstractions is to go low.

I live in NeoVim and Linux. Not because it's trendy — because it makes me think about what's actually happening on my machine. Same reason I climb: it demands full presence and honest feedback.

I'm curious, methodical, and interested in craft. I read a lot — mostly technical books on systems, compilers, and computer architecture — and I take notes.

📚 Currently reading: Stucture and Interpretation of Computer Programs
🧗 Climbs outdoors on weekends
